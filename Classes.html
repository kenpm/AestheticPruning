Merritt College Pruning Classes:

 

 

Merritt College Pruning Classes:

January
  
01/25/14 - 9:30am - 4:30pm
Introduction to Aesthetic Pruning:
Instructor: Chris Ingram
Introduction to aesthetic pruning of ornamental trees: Assessment of the tree and setting, timing and procedures for achieving a desired result.
Location: Merritt College Horticulture Department, Room H101
Fee: $78
Information: To register call 510-436-2418 or register in person at room H107 on campus 10 days in advance.
